## Recovery plan (if 30 days in with <500 followers)

This *will* happen for ~30% of niches. Symptoms: posts get 80–200 views forever, no organic spike.

Root-cause checklist (in order):
1. **Hook problem.** Are the first 1.5 seconds of every video boring? Re-edit to start at the most visually dramatic frame.
2. **Niche mismatch.** Search the niche hashtags — are your visuals visibly different from the top 50 posts? If they look the same, you're invisible. If they look too different, the algo can't categorise. Find the middle.
3. **Posting volume.** Are you actually shipping 3/day? Algo rewards velocity.
4. **AI disclosure problem.** Are you over-disclosing in the first 3 seconds and turning viewers off? Move the `#AI` to caption end + bio only.
5. **Wrong platform.** TikTok favours novel visual hooks; Pinterest favours consistency. If TT is flat but Pinterest is climbing, lean Pinterest-first for a month.
6. **Niche pivot.** If 3 of the 5 above are clean and still <500 followers at day 30, niche the niche down: from "dark academia" to "dark academia + occult tarot lore" or "dark academia + book reviews."

Don't pivot the persona name. Don't restart accounts. Don't delete content. Pivot only the content angle.
