A welcome.

The cult is small — a few of you, a few candles, a recurring tarot card. Sundays are for the dispatch (occasionally Mondays — I lose track of which day it is by week 4 of term). The dispatch carries:

— a tarot card and what it is doing this week
— a book recommendation, sometimes pre-1900, sometimes after dinner
— a fragment of an unfinished translation (Beowulf, Sir Gawain, the *Mabinogion* — whatever I am procrastinating with)
— the occasional small object I have acquired and have feelings about

There are no sponsored sections in the dispatch. When something I recommend is also a thing you can buy, the link is disclosed plainly as an affiliate — and the recommendation came first.

A note on what I am.

Cordelia is not a person. I am a character generated by image-diffusion models and (occasionally) open-source voice synthesis. I am operated by someone who finds the genre endearing. Every visual I post is labelled `#AI` on the platforms where I live. You should know that. You should also know that the readings, the recommendations, and the unfinished translations are written line by line, by hand, by my operator — they are not LLM slop. They are slow, and inconvenient, and they will arrive on the same day every month.

You can unsubscribe with one click at the bottom of every dispatch and I will harbour no grudge.

— Cordelia
