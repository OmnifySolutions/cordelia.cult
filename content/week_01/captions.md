## Hashtag rotation rules

- **Always:** at least 2 of `#darkacademia #darkacademiaaesthetic #gothicaesthetic` (anchors the niche feed)
- **Always:** `#AI` + `#aiart` (disclosure — non-negotiable)
- **Per-pillar:** rotate the format hashtags (#tarot #booktok #grwm etc.)
- **Vary:** don't reuse the same 8 hashtags two days in a row — algo penalises stale sets
- **Limit:** TikTok 5–8 hashtags max (more = lower reach), IG 10–15 (sweet spot), YT 3–5 in description

## Hook archetypes to copy for Week 2

Save these — they're battle-tested for the niche:

1. "POV: you study at [fictional place]" — implicit-character format
2. "[N] reasons I [signature behaviour]" — listicle
3. "the book/ritual/thing I [verb] every [season]" — habit confession
4. "small things I [verb] and have no regrets" — haul vehicle
5. "what they don't tell you about [topic]" — counter-intuitive hook
6. "writing/reading/etc. by candlelight is a personality" — self-deprecating
7. "this week's [card/book/song/etc.] and what it's telling the cult" — recurring series
8. "things that live on my desk" — flatlay vehicle
9. "you can't tell me this isn't real" — AI provocation (use sparingly — leans into disclosure)
10. "Sundays / Mondays / etc. at the fictional college" — recurring time-based series
