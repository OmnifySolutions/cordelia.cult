# Brand Outreach + Affiliate Request Templates

## When to send

- **Affiliate application (no follower minimum):** day 1, to every brand without a public affiliate network.
- **Gifted post pitch:** when you cross 3K followers on either TT or IG. Free product in exchange for one post + 24-hour story.
- **Paid post pitch:** when you cross 10K followers + 5%+ engagement rate.
- **Long-term partnership pitch:** when you have 3+ successful gifted posts logged.

## Format

- Send via Instagram DM to the brand's main account (not the founder's personal — looks like spam). Backup: brand email from their website footer.
- Don't attach a PDF media kit until they reply. First message = curiosity-bait, not pitch deck.
- Lead with the niche fit. Brands get 50 generic pitches a day. The AI-persona angle is your differentiator — own it.
