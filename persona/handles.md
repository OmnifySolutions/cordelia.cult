| Instagram | `https://www.instagram.com/HANDLE` | "Sorry, this page isn't available" |
| YouTube | `https://www.youtube.com/@HANDLE` | "This page isn't available" / 404 |
| Pinterest | `https://www.pinterest.co.uk/HANDLE` | "User not found" / generic page |
| Gumroad | `https://HANDLE.gumroad.com` | "Page not found" or NXDOMAIN |
| .com domain | `https://HANDLE.com` (or check Namecheap) | NXDOMAIN or "Buy this domain" |

Do not register on any platform until all 5 are clear on the *same* handle. Once they all check out, register everywhere in one session (within 30 min) — squatters watch new handles.

**Important:** handles with periods (`@atelier.nocturne`) sometimes need to be re-styled as `@ateliernocturne` on platforms that don't allow dots in the username. Pick a handle where both forms are available, ideally.
